Personal Experience:

5 Ingredient Brownie Recipe:

Ingredients:

  1. 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  2. 4 eggs
  3. 1/2 cup of sugar
  4. 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  5. 1/2 cup of flour

Steps to follow:

  1. In a bowl start by mixing all the dry ingredients.
  2. Then, incorporate the eggs and the vegetable oil
  3. Stir until they are well combined
  4. Pour the batter into a baking pan and bake for 20-30 minutes
